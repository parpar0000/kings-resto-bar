About Us

We at King's Resto Bar, located in Aurora, Zamboanga del Sur, are one of the favorite hangouts for beer and conversations. Our goal is to offer the best experience to our guests, ensuring an authentic beer chillout experience in a warm, friendly, and relaxed environment. We aim to bring a sense of relaxation to the city with our cozy space, complete with comfortable couches to lounge in while you enjoy your steak and beer.
